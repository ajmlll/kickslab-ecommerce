Return Policy & Rules

Please review our return guidelines

  • Returns are accepted within 7 days from delivery date.
  • Shoes must be unused and in original box and packaging.
  • Product should not have scratches, dirt, or wear marks.
  • Try shoes indoors before requesting return.
  • Return request is subject to admin approval.
  • Refund will be processed after product inspection.
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable.

Note: Shoes showing signs of use will not be accepted for return. Return approval may take 24–48 hours after request.

REQUEST SUBMITTED!

Your return request has been successfully received. We will review your request and contact you within 24-48 hours regarding the next steps.

What happens next?

  • Our team will inspect your evidence photos.
  • If approved, we'll arrange a reverse pickup.
  • Refund will be credited to your KICKSLAB Wallet after inspection.
